THE TECHNOLOGY

What It Is

The ROM³ Rehab System is a patented technology and medical protocol that dramatically improves and speeds lower extremity rehabilitation.

Who It's For

The ROM³ System can benefit nearly any patient after a lower extremity orthopedic injury or surgery, especially Knee or Hip Replacement.  It has also been shown to benefit patients with other ailments or conditions, including arthritis, stroke, heart disease, and many more.

How It Works

The ROM³ Rehab System involves a patented adjustable pedal system (X-Y Technology™) which enables patients with any lower extremity Range of Motion to immediately begin movement within a limited Range of Motion of the affected joint or limb.
