Purpose

To provide, worldwide, the most effective lower extremity rehabilitation system in the healthcare market – one that is easier for patients to use, yields better patient outcomes, speeds their rehabilitation times and overall recoveries, and saves money for patients, healthcare facilities, and third party providers.

COMPANY MANAGEMENT

Kenneth Johnson, M.D., MS, FAAPMR
Inventor, Founder,
Chief Technology Officer

A Doctor of Medicine with a Master’s Degree in Exercise Science, Dr. Johnson is a recognized expert and specialist in Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation, as well as a creative entrepreneur.

Since completing his residency at UCLA / West L.A. VA Medical Center, Dr. Johnson has helped thousands of patients through numerous leadership and hands-on roles in several medical facilities over the last 22 years, including as Medical Director overseeing Sports Medicine and Physical Medicine & Rehabilitation at US Healthworks, Newport Orthopedic Institute, and others.

He served as Director of both Outpatient and Inpatient Rehab Services at Labette County Medical Center, where he first developed, tested, and refined the ROM³. Other positions have included E.R. Physician and Board Certified Medical Examiner.

Dr. Johnson is passionate about using his knowledge to help others, and has pursued a variety of entrepreneurial endeavors, including educational services to help people create healthier lifestyles.

Sanford Gomberg
Co-Founder, CEO, Director

Mr. Gomberg brings over 30 years of experience as a seasoned executive and marketing professional, in healthcare as well as other industries.

Past executive management roles have included CEO, Chief Operating Officer, Chief Marketing Officer, and Vice President of Marketing, for startup, early stage, and well-established businesses.

Enjoying exposure to many different aspects of healthcare, Mr. Gomberg has successfully served several healthcare companies including Dubois Regional Medical Center, Whitaker Wellness Institute (largest U.S. alternative healthcare facility), Practice Optimizer (a physician practice management consulting firm), Hemorrhoid Relief Centers (10 clinics nationwide), and Adtenna Advertising Agency (U.S. ad agency for Bionorica).

Non-healthcare companies served have included ADDTEL (telecom), Verio (global web hosting leader) and Global Skies Bio Energy. Mr. Gomberg has received accolades for his design and management of successful marketing campaigns for several products, as well as for his leadership and implementation of best practices which have proven to establish and expand companies.
